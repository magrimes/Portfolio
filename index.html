About Me

Meredith Grimes earned a B.S. in Fashion Development and Product Management from NC State University with a minor in Art and Design. She was a Personal Stylist and Visual Merchandiser for three years.

Her passion for learning and eagerness to build on her academic foundations has led her to study Coding and Web Development at UNC Charlotte through their Continuing Education program.

Meredith believes that teamwork in the workplace is one of the key building blocks for success. She is currently working as a freelance designer and hopes to pursue a career as a web development professional. Contact her via email to connect.
